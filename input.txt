The tiger was once known to be the most feared animal in the forest. All the creatures avoided him, not only because of his strength, but also due to his cunning ways. One day, the tiger realized that his power had earned him nothing but loneliness. He decided to change. He began offering animals gifts—like golden bangles and sweet words—to win back their trust. However, his reputation made the animals wary. A wise fox warned others not to fall for his tricks. "No tiger changes overnight," he said. Despite this, one young deer approached the tiger out of curiosity.
